Overview

Welcome to Worktime, the website that provides real-time tracking of your job career! With our innovative platform, you can easily keep track of your progress and set goals for your professional development. But that's not all - we are excited to announce that soon we will be launching a Worktime app, making it even easier for you to track your career on-the-go. And that's not all, Worktime will soon be used by 1000+ hotels, making it the go-to platform for career development in the hospitality industry. Join the Worktime community today and take your career to the next level!

Mission

Our unwavering mission is to connect talented individuals with their dream jobs, while simultaneously providing businesses with top-notch employees. At WorkTime, we strive to foster a mutually beneficial relationship between job seekers and companies, creating a win-win scenario for all parties involved.

Goal

By the end of the year, our goal is to onboard 500 new businesses and connect them with qualified job seekers through our platform, helping to decrease unemployment rates and positively impact the economy.

History

Founded by Arkashish Sarma Bordoloi, WorkTime is a platform that originated from his father's vision. As an individual who often received phone calls from both HR and job seekers, Arkashish saw a need for a solution that would provide people with access to job opportunities and help HR departments find suitable candidates with ease. With this vision in mind, he set out to create WorkTime, a platform that seamlessly connects job seekers and employers. Today, WorkTime has evolved to include real-time tracking of job career progress and will soon be launching a companion app to further enhance the user experience.
